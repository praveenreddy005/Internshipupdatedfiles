Application Tracking

Candidates should be able to track their application through various stages. The process starts with profile building and document uploads, followed by approvals from the IBU and PSC heads. After securing these approvals, HR issues an offer letter. The onboarding process then begins, involving GID creation, mentor assignment, and project allocation.

This comprehensive tracking enhances transparency and communication but also ensures that the candidate is well-informed and prepared at every step of their integration into the organization.
